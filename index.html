Muhamamd Aasim Rafique

I am a faculty memeber in the department of computer science at King Faisal University. My areas of research include particularly machine learning in general artificial intelligence.

I have worked on self-driving vehicles, neuromorphic devices and computer vision during my stay at Machine Learning and Vision lab, where I spent time as a reseach professor, postdoc and a phd student.
